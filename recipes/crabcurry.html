Crab Legs in Coconut Curry

Description

This vibrant and aromatic dish combines the sweet, tender meat of crab legs with a rich, flavorful coconut curry. The curry sauce, infused with garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices, creates a delightful and exotic dining experience. Perfect for a special dinner, this recipe brings a taste of the tropics to your table.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of crab legs (king crab or snow crab)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Crab Legs:
    • Pre-cook the crab legs by steaming or boiling them for about 5 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Make the Curry Base:
    • Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
    • Add the finely chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    • Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another 2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add Spices:
    • Stir in the curry powder and turmeric, cooking for 1 minute to release their flavors.
  4. Add Liquids:
    • Pour in the coconut milk and chicken or vegetable broth, stirring to combine.
    • Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add Crab Legs:
    • Add the pre-cooked crab legs to the curry sauce.
    • Simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the crab legs to absorb the sauce.
  6. Finish the Sauce:
    • Stir in the fish sauce (if using) and lime juice. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Serve:
    • Transfer the crab legs and curry sauce to a serving dish.
    • Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro for a burst of color and freshness.
    • Serve immediately with cooked rice to soak up the delicious curry sauce.
