Steamed Crab Legs with Garlic Butter

Description

This classic recipe brings out the natural sweetness of crab legs, enhancing it with the rich, savory flavors of garlic and butter. Steaming the crab legs ensures they remain tender and juicy, while the garlic butter adds a deliciously aromatic touch. Perfect for a special occasion or a delightful seafood feast at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of crab legs (king crab or snow crab work well)
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of dry white wine (optional, can substitute with more water)
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Steamer:
    • In a large pot, add 1 cup of water and 1 cup of dry white wine. If you prefer not to use wine, you can use 2 cups of water instead.
    • Place a steamer basket or insert into the pot. Ensure the liquid level is below the bottom of the steamer basket to avoid direct contact with the crab legs.
  2. Steam the Crab Legs:
    • Bring the liquid to a boil over high heat
    • Once boiling, place the crab legs in the steamer basket. If they are too large, you may need to break them into smaller sections to fit.
    • Cover the pot with a lid and reduce the heat to medium-high.
    • Steam the crab legs for 5-7 minutes, or until they are heated through. You’ll know they’re ready when the shells are bright red and the meat is opaque.
  3. Prepare the Garlic Butter:
    • While the crab legs are steaming, melt 1/2 cup of unsalted butter in a small saucepan over low heat or in a microwave-safe bowl in the microwave.
    • Add the minced garlic to the melted butter and stir well.
    • Mix in 1 tablespoon of lemon juice to add a fresh, zesty flavor.
  4. Serve:
    • Carefully remove the steamed crab legs from the pot using tongs, and place them on a serving platter.
    • Drizzle the garlic butter generously over the crab legs.
    • Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color and a hint of freshness.
    • Serve the crab legs immediately with lemon wedges on the side for squeezing over the meat.
    • Offer any remaining garlic butter in small bowls for dipping.

Enjoy the meal!!
